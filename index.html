Portfolio

From an idea on paper to a fully-functional live site, below is a brief overview of how I approach each project.
Step 1: Research

Strategy

It is a great start to identify who your target audience is and for what reasons. At this stage, we want to identify their goals and our goals to ensure we fulfil this when we start designing.

Scope & Structure

Now that we know what are users want from us, we now need to identify what features are workable to develop. Also, I like to know how each information element fits together.

Step 2: Design

Skeleton

The fun now begins! We start designing what we like the website to look like. It is a great idea to hammer down the layout, functionality and style to ensure we meet user's goals.

Surface

At the surface stage, we decide what do we want to convey? What I mean is, what colours, font style are we going to use to help convey your brand story.

Step 3: Development

Code

This is the most exciting part of the project. It is where we develop from an idea to something real!
